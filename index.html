About Me

I am a Senior Technical Recruiter with over 9 years of experience specializing in US and Canada staffing across IT and engineering domains. I have a strong track record of delivering top-tier talent for Fortune 500 clients including Salesforce, Okta, Pinterest, DTCC, and Logitech. My expertise lies in full-cycle recruitment, from sourcing and screening to interview coordination, offer negotiation, and successful onboarding. I am highly proficient with ATS platforms like JobDiva and Ceipal, and I actively leverage job boards such as Dice, Monster, CareerBuilder, Indeed, LinkedIn Recruiter, and Techfetch to identify both active and passive candidates. I have extensive experience working with VMS platforms including Fieldglass, VNDLY, Beeline, IQN, and Magnit, enabling me to efficiently manage high-volume and priority requirements. I focus on building strong talent pipelines, maintaining excellent client and candidate relationships, and consistently delivering quality hires within tight timelines. My approach combines data-driven recruitment strategies with a deep understanding of market trends, ensuring successful placements and long-term client satisfaction.
